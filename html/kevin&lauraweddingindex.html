The Story of Kevin & Laura...

The Meeting

November 8th, 2013: Two strangers go to a party in Isla Vista with their friends- one a senior at UCSB and the other an out-of-towner visiting from the Marine Corps base in Twenty-Nine Palms. One could call it a typical night, until two pairs of eyes caught each other through the crowd. Over their red solo cup cheers and spilt beer, Kevin and Laura introduced themselves and something stuck. Chatting over a keg at a college party, it was obvious there was a spark between the two. Phone numbers were exchanged and the two parted ways, only to run into each other a second time elsewhere before the night would end. Coincidence perhaps? A fateful one. That night the first of a billion texts were exchanged between the two. Over the next couple of days, plans were made for the following weekend, and the one after that. The annual Marine Corps Ball in Las Vegas was coming up, would you like to go with me? Yes, I would quite like that. And just like that, things were instantly serious. Only a couple of weeks after meeting, Laura brought Kevin home for Thanksgiving to meet her family. And then a month later, Laura went to Iowa to meet Kevin's family. And on it went.

Fast Forward...

A year and a half later, one deployment, a million hours of Skyping, tons of weekends of traveling to see each other, two moves, and a puppy later, Kevin and Laura go on a hike to St. Mary's Glacier. After climbing up the mountain and hiking around the lake, Kevin and Laura sit by the water for a picnic of Gatorade and PB&J's. On one knee and locked eyes, a ring, a yes, and hugs and kisses finally sealed the deal.
